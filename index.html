Datalove

Activity Tracking

I started developing this app in the summer of 2013 to replace an app that was discontinued and getting unstable. I wanted something to easily keep track of my triathlon training and came up with a concept to easily track and visualize activities of any kind.

View this project on the App Store

Stronger

Weight Lifting

Weight lifting can be pretty complex. When I started, I noticed that simply tracking my workouts with Datalove is not nearly enough. Many existing apps weren't as beautiful and simple as i would like them to be. So I set out to develop a minimal, straight-forward app that featured strength routines of the same qualities.

fileee

Document Management

Between my Bachelor and Master I worked at a startup some of my friends founded. I developed the mobile concept, the iPhone and iPad app and helped with the API of the server. The most challenging aspects were working with OpenCV and C++ to improve camera pictures of documents and creating a reliable offline sync mechanism.
